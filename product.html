Three modules

Product

TwinBridge is organized into three modules that cover prognosis, control construction, and safe adaptation without revealing proprietary data sources.

A) Prognosis twins

We model progression trajectories and time-to-event outcomes with evaluation designed for label validity, calibration, subgroup reliability, and distribution shift.

B) Synthetic control arms (Phase II/III)

We build matched comparison groups that approximate what outcomes would look like without treatment, then compare those estimates to randomized evidence where available. We only operate within validated support and with explicit bias accounting.

C) Pretrain → adapt

We develop pretrained disease-dynamics models and use bounded local adaptation (e.g., recalibration/shrinkage) under a strict re-audit policy.

What stays public

  • Model-agnostic claims and transparent decision gates.
  • Evidence expectations and failure modes.
  • Clear scope for when outputs are decision-ready.
