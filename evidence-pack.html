We frame evidence as a mapping from claim to required validation, consistent with a pre-registered protocol mindset.

Minimal Evidence Pack (public outline)

  • Reference standard & timing validity (what is known vs. uncertain).
  • Calibration + uncertainty reporting (not AUROC-only).
  • Leakage / clinical realism checks.
  • Generalizability: temporal/site stress tests; subgroup calibration.
  • For synthetic controls: overlap/positivity + effect-bias benchmarking vs RCT.

A concise 1-page PDF can be provided on request to describe these categories without numerical claims.
