TwinBridge

Digital-twin synthetic control arms with public-health safeguards for decision-ready Phase II/III evidence.

TwinBridge builds digital-twin models that support synthetic control arms and prognosis in neurodegenerative disease, while explicitly quantifying when results are reliable under shift, bias, and uncertainty.

What we do

Natural-history twins

Trajectory modeling and time-to-event endpoints in neurodegenerative disease.

Synthetic controls

Augment or partially replace placebo arms when acceptability gates are met.

Decision-readiness safeguards

Overlap/positivity, calibration/fidelity, and causal plausibility checks.

Integrity

  • Not universal control-arm replacement.
  • Not portability without passing predefined gates.
  • Not a clinical decision tool for individual patient care.

Why it matters

When trials are small and endpoints are hard to measure, the risk is not only bias—it is overconfidence. Our focus is decision readiness: clear conditions for when evidence is strong enough to act, and when it is not.

Mission

TwinBridge focuses on epi-grade validity and decision readiness for neurodegenerative trials. We pair with pharma teams, hospitals, and natural history data companies to develop drug-specific solutions.
