Disease Focus

TwinBridge prioritizes diseases where decision readiness is hardest and safeguards are most needed.

ALS

Rapid progression, heterogeneous trajectories, and small trials with high unmet need.

Dementia

Long disease course, diverse phenotypes, and strong need for transportability across sites.

Parkinson’s

Slow progression with motor and non-motor outcomes that benefit from longitudinal modeling.
